About PixelPop Studio

Your premier destination for digital photobooth experiences

What We Offer

PixelPop Studio is a state-of-the-art web-based photobooth application designed to bring professional photography experiences to your fingertips. Our platform combines cutting-edge web technologies with intuitive design to create memorable photo sessions.

How to Use PixelPop Studio

1

Navigate to Photo Booth

Click on "Photo Booth" in the navigation menu or use the "Start Capturing" button on the home page.

2

Choose Your Layout

Select from 4 different photo layouts including single, collage, strip, and grid options.

3

Customize Your Photos

Pick from various borders and apply creative filters to enhance your photos.

4

Set Timer & Capture

Use the timer feature to get ready for your shot, then capture your perfect moment.

5

Download & Print

Save your completed photo session and print or share your memories.
