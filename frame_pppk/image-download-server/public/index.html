All About PixelPop Studio

PixelPop Studio is a state-of-the-art web-based photobooth application designed to bring professional photography experiences to your fingertips. Whether you’re capturing solo shots, group selfies, or creating fun layouts with friends, our platform combines cutting-edge web technologies with intuitive design to create memorable photo sessions. With customizable layouts, creative filters, and instant downloads, you can turn everyday moments into lasting memories—no extra apps or complicated tools required.

How to Use PixelPop Studio

1

Login or Register

Sign in to unlock downloads, printing, and saving to My Gallery. Guests can still capture photos but cannot save them.

2

Navigate to Photo Booth

Click on Photo Booth in the navigation menu or use the Start Capturing button on the home page.

3

Choose Your Layout

Select from four options: Single, Two-Strip, Three-Strip, or Four-Strip layouts.

4

Customize Your Photos

Add borders and filters to match your style before capturing.

5

Set Timer & Capture

Use the built-in timer to prepare, then click the camera button to take photos.

6

Save & Manage

After capturing, you can Download, Print, or Save to My Gallery (requires login). Each user can store up to 50 photos in their gallery.

7

Access Your Gallery

Go to My Gallery to view, download, or delete your saved photos anytime.

Features

  • Real-time camera preview
  • Multiple photo layouts
  • Professional filters and effects
  • Customizable borders
  • Timer functionality
  • High-quality downloads
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Personal gallery with up to 50 saved photos

Tips & Troubleshooting

  • Allow camera permission in your browser if video doesn’t start.
  • For best results, use good lighting and keep your device steady.
  • On mobile, use portrait orientation for cleaner layouts.
  • Remember: downloading, printing, and gallery saving require login.
